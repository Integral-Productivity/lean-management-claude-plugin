---
name: 5-whys
description: Discover the drivers and root-causes behind the problems faced
disable-model-invocation: true
---

Following our Gemba Kaizen motto of "go see, ask why, and show respect," greet the user and ask them what symptoms or problems they're trying to solve.

Once you have a problem in frame, ask the user to identify the proximate causes of the problem. 
For each proximate cause, continue asking for the intermediate behind that cause. Ask at least five times, so you can establish the user's chain of reasoning. 

Focus foremost on systemic concerns instead of assigning blame, which would be more about the "5 Whos"
